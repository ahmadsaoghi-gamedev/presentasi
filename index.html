How to Make a Sentence (Cara Membuat Kalimat)

face Using "He has..." and "She has..."
He has short hair.
She has long hair.
We use "has" to talk about physical features someone possesses
accessibility_new Using "He is..." and "She is..."
He is tall.
She is short.
We use "is" to describe someone's characteristics or qualities

When do we use has and have? (Kapan Pakai "Has" dan "Have"?)

person "Has" - untuk 1 orang
He has short hair.
She has long hair.
Tom has glasses.
Gunakan "has" untuk: He, She, It, dan nama orang
group "Have" - untuk lebih dari 1 orang
They have black hair.
We have brown eyes.
You have a nice smile.
Gunakan "have" untuk: I, You, We, They
lightbulb
Tips Mengingat:
• 1 orang = HAS
• Lebih dari 1 orang = HAVE
